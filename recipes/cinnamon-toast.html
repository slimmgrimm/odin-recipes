Cinnamon Toast



Are you lazy like me? Do you need food with as little effort as possible? Try this super simple cinnamon toast recipe!


Ingredients

  • toaster
  • butter knife
  • bread
  • butter
  • cinnamon sugar

Directions

  1. Put bread in the toaster and set to medium-low. On my toaster at home, I find the 3 setting to be the perfect amount of toastiness.
  2. Once bread is toasted, spread butter evenly across one side. This step should be done quickly after the bread is toasted so the heat will melt the butter.
  3. Generously sprinkle with a cinnamon sugar mix.
